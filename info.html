The Darville Podcast is a weekly audio show hosted by Falon Darville. Falon is a writer and web developer who writes about culture, social media, creativity, and the workplace. She is interested in alternative workplaces like cooperatives and non-hierarchical structures. Falon is generally critical of the current realm of work and believes that working does not have to be as mentally and emotionally draining as it currently tends to be in the American workplace.

In regards to culture, Falon is interested in getting people thinking beyond the scope of their current media sources and their surroundings. She believes that education, via free and open sources especially, is the ultimate gift that you can give yourself. Falon also holds that we need to be more skeptical when it comes to social media. We have become a society of selecting representation of ourselves, and the adverse effects of this include relinquishing authenticity.

The Darville Podcast is meant to be a space for thinking. It is not meant to dictate absolutes, or even to convince anyone of a particular opinion. Rather, Falon's mission is to bring awareness to talking openly about things we generally don't think a great deal about, but that play a large role in our lives. It's important to question the way that we think, and where our thoughts come from. Through this, we can grow and develop our own opinions and ideas. We can also more easily recognize when we believe something simply by being told that it is the truth.
